About MASAIC

MASAIC represents a groundbreaking initiative in the field of robotics and artificial intelligence. Our mission is to develop next-generation autonomous systems that leverage the power of Large Language Models (LLMs) and multi-agent collaboration to solve complex real-world challenges.

Through interdisciplinary research and innovation, we aim to create intelligent robotic systems that can understand, reason, and interact naturally with humans and their environment.
