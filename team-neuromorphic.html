Overview

The Neuromorphic Computing Team pioneers brain-inspired computing architectures that revolutionize how robots process information. By emulating the structure and function of biological neural networks, we develop energy-efficient, adaptive systems capable of real-time learning and decision-making in dynamic environments.

Research Focus

Our research spans spiking neural networks (SNNs), neuromorphic hardware integration, event-based sensing, and bio-inspired learning algorithms. We focus on developing computational models that combine the efficiency of biological brains with the scalability of modern computing, enabling robots to achieve cognitive capabilities while maintaining low power consumption.
