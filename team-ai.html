Overview

The AI & LLM Integration Team is at the forefront of incorporating cutting-edge large language models into robotic systems. We develop frameworks that enable robots to understand, reason, and communicate using natural language, making human-robot collaboration more intuitive and effective.

Research Focus

Our work centers on bridging the gap between advanced language models and robotic control systems. We create innovative architectures that allow LLMs to interpret complex instructions, plan tasks, and make intelligent decisions in real-time robotic applications.
