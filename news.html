22 Oct 2025
Award

Assistant Professor Nguyen Huu Nhan Receives IEEE RAS Japan Young Award at IROS 2025

We are proud to announce that Assistant Professor Nguyen Huu Nhan from our Robotics & Control Team has been honored with the IEEE Robotics and Automation Society Japan Joint Chapter Young Award at IROS 2025. This prestigious recognition is given to outstanding young researchers for their exceptional contributions to the field of robotics and automation. His award-winning paper, "ViZTaP: A Cross-Polarization Based Mechanism for Perception Transition in Tactile-Proximity Sensing With Applications to Soft Grippers," demonstrates innovative research in tactile sensing technology.
