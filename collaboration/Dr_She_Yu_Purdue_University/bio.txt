Dr. She Yu is an Assistant Professor at Purdue University.

Current Position:
- Assistant Professor, School of Industrial Engineering, Purdue University

Research Interests:
Dr. Yu's research focuses on machine learning and intelligent systems. Her work encompasses the development of advanced algorithms and methodologies for intelligent decision-making systems, with applications in manufacturing, operations research, and automation.

Her research contributes to advancing the field of intelligent systems through innovative approaches to machine learning and optimization.
