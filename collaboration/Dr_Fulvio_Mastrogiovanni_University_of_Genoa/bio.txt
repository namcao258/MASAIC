Dr. Fulvio Mastrogiovanni is an Associate Professor at the University of Genoa, Italy.

Current Position:
- Associate Professor, University of Genoa

Research Interests:
Dr. Mastrogiovanni's research focuses on human-robot interaction and cognitive robotics. His work encompasses the development of intelligent robotic systems capable of understanding and responding to human behavior, with emphasis on cognitive architectures and interaction paradigms.

His research contributes to advancing the field of robotics through innovative approaches to human-robot collaboration and intelligent system design.
