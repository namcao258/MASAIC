Overview

The Human-Robot Interaction Team focuses on designing intuitive, natural, and effective ways for humans to interact with robotic systems. We develop multimodal interfaces that combine speech, gesture, and visual feedback to create seamless collaboration experiences.

Research Focus

Our research encompasses natural language interaction, gesture recognition, social robotics, and adaptive user interfaces. We study human behavior patterns and design interaction paradigms that make robots more accessible, trustworthy, and effective as collaborative partners.
