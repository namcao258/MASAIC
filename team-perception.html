Overview

The Perception & Sensing Team develops advanced systems that enable robots to perceive and understand their environment. Through cutting-edge computer vision, sensor fusion, and machine learning techniques, we create robust perception systems for autonomous operation.

Research Focus

Our research encompasses visual perception, 3D scene understanding, object detection and tracking, and multi-sensor integration. We develop algorithms that process data from cameras, LiDAR, radar, and other sensors to create comprehensive environmental models for intelligent decision-making.
